Carbonara

Description

This carbonara recipe is creamy, flavorful, and a quick dish to whip up. It's made with simple ingredients for a rich and indulgent meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bacon or pancetta
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles until al dente and reserve some pasta water.
  2. Fry the bacon or pancetta until crispy and set aside.
  3. Mix eggs and grated Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine the cooked noodles, egg mixture, and bacon in a pan over low heat.
  5. Stir in reserved pasta water for a creamy consistency.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve warm.
