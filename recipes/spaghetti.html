Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a classic comfort food with a rich and flavorful tomato sauce. It's simple to make and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles until al dente and set aside.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce by browning the ground meat, then adding onion and garlic.
  3. Stir in tomato products, sugar, and seasonings. Simmer the sauce.
  4. Combine the cooked noodles and sauce.
  5. Serve warm with grated Parmesan cheese.
